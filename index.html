Paper, Scissors, Rock

This is the game I developed after i finished the Codecademy tutorial of JavaScript.

For now, you are only able to play 1 Player game only, which means you play against your computer.

Click on each of the 3 images depending on your desired choice. When you make your choice, the computer chooses randomly paper, scissors or rock.
When, either you or the computer reaches the 3 trophies then the round is finished.
